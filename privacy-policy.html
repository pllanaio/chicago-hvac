Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026. This page explains how Chicago HVAC handles information submitted through our website, phone, email, and service request channels.

Information We Collect

When you contact Chicago HVAC, submit a service request, call us, or email us, we may collect information needed to respond to your request.

  • Name and contact details
  • Phone number and email address
  • Service address or general location
  • HVAC service details, equipment issues, urgency, and appointment notes
  • Technical website information such as browser type, device type, and basic usage data

How We Use Information

We use collected information to respond to inquiries, schedule HVAC service, provide estimates, communicate about appointments, improve website performance, and maintain business records.

Information Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when needed to provide services, operate our website, comply with legal obligations, protect our business, or communicate with service-related vendors.

Third-Party Services

Our website and business operations may use third-party services such as email providers, hosting providers, analytics tools, Instagram, and map or location services. These providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably necessary for customer communication, service records, legal compliance, and business operations.

Your Choices
